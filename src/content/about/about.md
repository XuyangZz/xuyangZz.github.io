I'm **Xuyang Zhou**, an M.Eng. student at the University of Electronic Science
and Technology of China (UESTC), working on **multi-modal large models and
embodied AI** — specifically, how to push vision-language-action systems
beyond narrow task demos toward something that generalizes.

Before UESTC I did a year as a research assistant at Shandong University and
a B.Eng. in Communication Engineering there. I've also spent a summer fine-tuning
LLMs for China Railway and a spring building vision systems for industrial defect
detection. The throughline across all of it: I like problems where the right
answer isn't obvious from the data alone.

Outside the lab, I try to spend as much time as I can with people who think
differently from me. I taught myself English without a tutor, hosted visiting
professors from Lisbon at my campus, spent a week as a visiting scholar at
Khalifa University in Abu Dhabi, and am currently tutoring a high-schooler
in spoken English. I write about some of those things here.

If you want to chat — about research, about learning a language, about
building robots that actually help people — feel free to
[email me](mailto:202521011742@std.uestc.edu.cn) or find me on
[GitHub](https://github.com/xuyangZz).
